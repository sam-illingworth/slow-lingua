## What Was Learned

### Phrases
- Je m'appelle ___ (My name is ___)
- J'habite à ___ (I live in ___)
- Je suis ___ (I am ___ / profession)
- J'aime ___ / J'adore ___ (I like / I love ___)
- Le matin/l'après-midi/le soir, je ___ (In the morning/afternoon/evening, I ___)

### Vocabulary
- 35 words added (see vocabulary.md)

### Grammar
- Elision: je + vowel = j'
- No article for professions
- Plural articles for general likes
- Verb endings: je = no 's'
